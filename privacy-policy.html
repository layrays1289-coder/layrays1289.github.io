We do not collect personal identification information directly. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect limited information necessary to provide their services, including advertising and app functionality.

The App uses Google AdMob as the primary advertisement provider and Unity Ads as a mediation partner.

3. In-App Purchases (Optional)

The App offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to permanently remove advertisements. All payments are securely processed by Google Play Billing. We do not collect, store, or have access to users’ payment or financial information.

4. Local Storage

The App uses local storage (via SharedPreferences) to save game progress, high scores, language selection, and sound settings. This data remains local on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

5. Target Audience and Children’s Privacy

The App is designed for a general audience and is suitable for children aged 6 and above. We comply with Google Play’s Families Policy and applicable child privacy regulations, including COPPA.
