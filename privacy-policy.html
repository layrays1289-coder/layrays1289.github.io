Privacy Policy for Math Games: Speed & Skill

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Math Games: Speed & Skill ("we", "our", or "the App") handles user information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect personal identification information directly. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect limited information necessary to provide their services.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

To ensure a safe experience for all users, including children aged 6 and above, the App is configured to show only family-friendly and non-personalized ads.

AdMob may use device identifiers for ad delivery and fraud prevention purposes in accordance with Google policies.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. In-App Purchases (Optional)

The App offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to permanently remove advertisements.

All payments are securely processed by Google Play Billing.

We do not collect, store, or have access to users’ payment or financial information.

The purchase is associated with the user's Google Play account and can be restored on supported devices.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

The App uses local storage (via SharedPreferences) to save game progress, high scores, language selection, and sound settings on the user’s device.

This data remains local and is not transmitted to our servers or third parties.

5. Target Audience and Children’s Privacy

The App is designed for a general audience and is suitable for children aged 6 and above.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

The App complies with Google Play’s Families Policy and applicable child privacy regulations, including COPPA.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services – for core app functionality
  • Google AdMob – for displaying advertisements

These services may collect data as governed by their respective privacy policies.

7. Security

As the App does not collect personal user data directly, data security is primarily managed by the Android operating system and trusted third-party providers such as Google.
